# Sound Files for HauntWrite

Place the following sound files in this directory:

## Required Files:

1. **ambient.mp3** - Low volume ambient whisper/static sound (looping)
   - Suggested: Subtle wind, whispers, or static noise
   - Volume: Very low (will be set to 10% in code)

2. **click.mp3** - Short haunting click sound for buttons
   - Suggested: Subtle "whoosh" or ghostly whisper
   - Duration: < 0.5 seconds

3. **haunt.mp3** - Haunting sound for "Haunt My Entry" button
   - Suggested: Eerie chord, ghost sound, or supernatural effect
   - Duration: 1-2 seconds

## Where to find sounds:
- freesound.org
- zapsplat.com
- pixabay.com (royalty-free)

## Note:
The app will work without these files, but sounds will be silent.
Audio playback failures are handled gracefully.
